Privacy Policy

VoiceClone AAC — Augmentative & Alternative Communication

1. Who We Are

VoiceClone AAC is developed and operated by Aher Technologies Private Limited, a company registered in India. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@voicecloneaac.app.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address and display name when you register.
  • Voice recordings: short audio samples (up to 15 seconds) you provide to create your cloned voice. These are uploaded securely and used solely for voice synthesis.
  • Phrases: text phrases you create and save inside the app.
  • Usage data: phrase use counts and timestamps, used to surface recently used phrases.
  • On-device cache: synthesized audio files stored locally on your device for offline use. This data never leaves your device except as described below.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and serve your personalized cloned voice via ElevenLabs.
  • To store and sync your phrase library across sessions.
  • To provide offline playback of previously synthesized phrases.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • ElevenLabs – Your voice recording and synthesis requests are processed by ElevenLabs to create your voice clone. ElevenLabs is subject to their own Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase – We use Supabase for authentication and database storage. Data is stored in Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL. See their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your account data and phrases are retained as long as your account is active. Voice recordings submitted to ElevenLabs are governed by ElevenLabs' retention policy. You may delete your local on-device cache at any time from Settings → Storage → Clear Cache. To delete your account and all server-side data, contact us at privacy@voicecloneaac.app.

6. Children's Privacy

VoiceClone AAC is designed for adults with communication needs (e.g. ALS, stroke). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has registered, contact us immediately and we will delete the account.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for voice processing at any time.

Contact privacy@voicecloneaac.app to exercise any of these rights.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. Access to your data is protected by Row Level Security policies — no user can access another user's data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
